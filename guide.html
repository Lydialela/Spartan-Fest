Policies

  • NO Re-entry/ins & outs per day
  • NO Camping
  • NO Cash: HARD Summer is a cashless event. Credit, debit, Samsung Pay, Apple
    Pay, and Android Pay will be accepted.
  • NO Refunds or exchanges
  • NO Sitting on designated dancefloor areas
  • NO Moshing, crowd-surfing or stage-diving
  • NO Unauthorized/unlicensed vendors
  • NO Unauthorized solicitations, handbills, sampling, giveaways, etc.



Health and Accessibility

Number of guests is being limited due to current pandemic concerns. Dedicated team of Care Bears (people readily available to assist festival goers) will be roaming the festival grounds ready to help! Professional team of Medical Staff and Medical Tents will be present and available to every guest. All restrooms will be cleaned thoroughly and fully stocked. IDâ€™s will be checked at entry points

Covid Regulations:

Masks will not be required. Proof of vaccination will be required to participate in the festival. We encourage our guests to use the CaliforniaSafe app to verify vaccination status or to bring their vaccination cards. If guests can not show proof of vaccination, two negative tests must be shown as proof of health. Tests must be taken within 3-4 days of the event and vitals will be checked at entrance.

We will not tolerate the following:

  • Possession/use/sale of illegal drugs.
  • Irresponsible or unsafe use of alcohol.
  • Fighting or actions that may harm or endanger anyone.
  • Damage, destruction, vandalism or theft of any property.



Frequently Asked Questions

When festival starts/ends?

July 16th and 17th at 2 pm - 10 pm.

Where can I park?

Parking will be available on site and at our off-site lot.

What is the max number of tickets I can buy?

Max number of tickets one guest can purchase is 4 tickets.

What are the food option at the festival?

Our food vendors will congregate at a common dining area where guests can enjoy a range of foods.

What payment methods can be used to purchase tickets?

Debit card, credit card, cash, checks, apple pay/google pay/samsung pay. Checks need to be mailed a month in advance.
